KUYA A HARDWARE was established in January of 2020, located on Cogeo, Antipolo City.

This establishment provides high-quality hardware and construction materials, serving contractors, builders, architects, and DIY enthusiasts.

The products range from: Building materials, Hardware and tools, Plumbing and Electrical, Interior and Exterior Finishes. At KUYA A HARDWARE, quality is non-negotiable. The company sources its products from reputable manufacturers known for their superior craftsmanship and adherence to industry standards. Every product in the inventory undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure it meets the company’s criteria for performance and durability.

The mission at KUYA A HARDWARE is to empower customers with the tools and materials they need to bring their construction visions to life.

Through dedication to quality, service, and innovation, the company aims to be the preferred partner for all construction and hardware needs, contributing to the success of every project it serves.
